Questions and Answers about STO

What is Credits’ STO?

These are shares in the company that are structured in the form of security tokens on the blockchain. Each token is backed by shares in Credits Digital Corp. Unicorn Tokenization Corp. acts as the issuer of the tokens, behind which Credits company’s stocks are held.
These tokens are similar to securities or ETFs. Tokens/shares have a denomination, circulation period and a maturity date. Read more here.
